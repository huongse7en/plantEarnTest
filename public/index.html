Hero

In Summoners Arena, Heroes are powerful characters represented as tradeable NFT collectibles.

Stronger Heroes beat others more easily and earn rarer resources.

Items

Valuable Items enhance Heroes with unmatched power to dominate the battlefield.

Three same items can be merged to upgrade into a rarer one.

Guild

Guild Leaderships are NFTs that make owners Guild Leaders. Leaders can recruit Members and lead the Guilds to Guild Wars, Titan Fights and Dungeons.

Guild Members have exclusive perks and the chance to win rare Guild Items.

Land

Lands are limited NFTs that make their owners Landlords.

Lands have Dungeons associated with them and Guilds can pay Landlords with $SEG to rent these Dungeons. Rare items and other resources can be obtained by exploring Dungeons.
